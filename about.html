ABOUT US

Last Mile (LM) Impact Consultancy is a female-driven, client-orientated firm offering integrated communication solutions and project management services. LM Impact uses holistic, and result-based approaches to support a variety of organisation types including private and public institutions.

Although tactical steps can be sufficient to address the needs of a project, our experience in delivering large programs has demonstrated the importance of developing a clear communications strategy. Within a large program of work, there is a vast amount of communication that needs to be exchanged across a broad range of stakeholders. One of the key challenges is ensuring appropriate distribution of key communications from project leadership to impacted team members and beneficiaries within the program while recognizing and preventing communication overload.

The LM Impact team has a wealth of experience in strategic communications, operations management, resource mobilisation, advocacy, partnership management, media relations and content production.

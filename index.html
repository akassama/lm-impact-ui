Services

LM Impact takes a 360-degree approach to communications and ensures that programs have the desired impact. This includes:

INTERNAL PROGRAM COMMUNICATION

What is happening in the day-to-day delivery of the program and what changes or successes/delays team members need to be aware of.

COMMUNICATION (PROGRAM - CUSTOMER)

The best way for the program to communicate across more than one business organization or community, for example with donor-funded projects.

EXTERNAL COMMUNICATION

What information is appropriate for media relations and when best to share it.

CORPORATE COMMUNICATION

Engagement with internal corporate centres and organizations.

CONTENT EDITING

Proof reading, reviewing, plan and organise content for technical and academic writing.
